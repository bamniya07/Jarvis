Here is a sample application for sick leave:

**Application for Sick Leave**

**To:** [Name of Supervisor/HR Department]

**From:** [Your Name]

**Date:** [Current Date]

**Subject:** Request for Sick Leave

Dear [Supervisor's Name],

I am writing to request a sick leave of [number of days] days from [start date] to [end date] due to health reasons.

Unfortunately, I am currently unwell and am unable to come to work. I am experiencing [briefly mention your illness or symptoms, e.g. fever, flu, stomach problem, etc.]. I am undergoing treatment and am expected to recover within the next [number of days] days.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. I will provide a medical certificate from my doctor if required.

I would appreciate it if you could approve my request for sick leave. If there are any issues or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

**Note:** You can modify this template as per your company's policies and requirements. Also, be sure to keep a copy of the application for your records.
